Art History Flashcards

Who painted the "Starry Night"?
Vincent van Gogh
What art movement is known for its focus on dreams and the subconscious?
Surrealism
Which famous sculpture depicts a biblical hero before his battle with a giant?
David (by Michelangelo)
What iconic artwork features a woman with a mysterious smile?
Mona Lisa
Which artistic technique involves applying thick layers of paint?
Impasto
What is the name of Andy Warhol's famous series featuring soup cans?
Campbell's Soup Cans
From what ancient civilization does the Great Sphinx originate?
Ancient Egypt
What architectural style is characterized by pointed arches and stained glass windows?
Gothic
