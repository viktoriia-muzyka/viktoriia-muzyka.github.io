Steven Universe

The optimistic and empathetic half-human, half-Gem leader of the Crystal Gems. Steven is known for his powerful healing abilities and unwavering belief in the good in everyone

Garnet

The strong fusion of the Gems Ruby and Sapphire. Her future vision and immense physical power make her a formidable force and a pillar of the team

Amethyst

The free-spirited and shapeshifting Gem who emerged from the Kindergarten on Earth. Amethyst is known for her playful nature, her ability to transform into various forms, and her deep loyalty to her teammates

Pearl

The elegant and precise Gem who served under Rose Quartz. Pearl is highly skilled in combat, organization, and technology
